About Us

Founded in 2023 by Frank Onyema Orji

NatureBlog is a nonprofit, volunteer-run site dedicated to raising awareness of important. NatureBlog was founded in 2023 with the mission of raising awareness of important wildlife and ocean conservation issues. We are passionate about protecting our Earth's natural environments and the incredible creatures that call them home. Through engaging blog posts, photography, and video, our team of writers, naturalists and marine biologists hope to both educate readers and inspire action.


Our founding blogger, Frank Orji, has always had a deep love of nature since his childhood days exploring the forests near his home. He is studying Software Engineering in college and spent a year volunteering at an Africa wildlife preserve. It was there that he witnessed firsthand the threats facing many wild species and habitats. He was struck by how interconnected our world is - how the decline of one animal could impact entire ecosystems. Frank was also moved by the dedication of rangers and conservationists working tirelessly to make a difference, often with scarce resources.


When Frank returned home, he wanted to find a way to contribute to conservation efforts from afar. Combining his writing skills with his passion for the natural world, he launched NatureBlog. Here Frank shares stories from the field, highlights neglected or imperiled species, and reports on the cutting-edge research and policies that could reshape our relationship with the planet. He also profiles inspiring individuals making an impact through their conservation work.


NatureBlog also features the work of young emerging voices in conservation. Through our Campus Correspondents program, we publish articles and reports from college students conducting field research or environmental advocacy campaigns. We hope to nurture the next generation of conservation leaders and give them an platform to raise awareness of their important work.


As an entirely nonprofit and volunteer-run site, our mission remains to utilize the power of storytelling and digital media to foster greater understanding and action. All profits from NatureBlog's sponsors and donors go directly towards supporting frontline environmental groups and community-based conservation efforts in places like Africa, Southeast Asia and South America. We also maintain a list of reputable organizations on our site where readers can donate or volunteer their time if they feel inspired to support a particular cause or region after reading one of our stories.


So whether you are an enthusiast wanting to deepen your knowledge or simply curious about our fragile planet, we hope you will find something compelling here at NatureBlog. Our goal is to astonish with imagery of threatened landscapes, educate on pressing issues, and ignite that flame of care for nature - so together we can work to safeguard Earth's biodiversity for generations to come.
